## Forensic Geosimulation: Finding the Factual Boundary of Road Accidents

**Category:** Urban Simulation · **Status:** Completed

A custom simulation for forensic spatial analysis, designed to determine the physical avoidability of a right-turn vehicle-pedestrian collision based on geometric constraints and probabilistic human factors. This project extends geosimulation principles into a high-fidelity, short-temporal-window investigative pipeline.

**Tech:** Python · AnyLogic · GIS · Java

- [View Project](https://github.com/snigdha4603/ASA-final-project)
- [GitHub](https://github.com/snigdha4603/ASA-final-project)

## Virtual Reality Study of Pedestrian-Motor vehicle conflicts at Right-turn Intersections 

**Category:** Computer Vision, Deep Learning, Ethnographic Study · **Status:** Ongoing


This project focuses on conducting an ethnographic study of right-turn conflicts at dangerous New York City intersections using computer vision and virtual reality. Developed a custom labeling tool to analyze vehicle and pedestrian interactions from recorded video, which will ultimately inform a time-based risk function and a simulated VR environment to provide policy recommendations.

**Tech:** Python · Unity · yolo26 · EEG
